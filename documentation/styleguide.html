Styleguide

General guidelines

  • Remove spaces in folder names and filenames

  • Remove hyphens in folder names and filenames

  • Make consistent filenames with PascalCase names for all .adoc files

  • Avoid having more than 1 blank line anywhere in an AsciiDoc document.

  • Avoid having trailing spaces at the end of any line in an AsciiDoc document. You can turn on the Trim Trailing Whitespace setting in VSCode to do this.

  • Lines over 100 characters have been split into multiple lines.
